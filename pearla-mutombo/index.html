Welcome to my page!

My first memory of using a computer was when I was my first year in middle school. My story starts when I was 8 years old, my mother had died of pneumonia. Leaving me and my father alone. As a single parent my father sought it best to leave me in the care of my grandparents take care of me. Since he was busy with work and travels. My family is originally from Kinshsa, which is the capital of the Democratic Republic of Congo. When I was growing up I didn't use any computer, perhaps because my parents couldn't afford it the part of the world where we're in.

It wasn't until I was in middle school that I entered the world of computers I got to use a computer in school through a school project. My family and I came to the United states when I was in 5th grade. Upon arrival to a new country, I had to adapt to a different culture and language. which was challenging at first for a minor but also exciting. Once, I masted the language it opened my mind and eyes to explore with different things. Using a computer opened up a whole new world of possibilities for me. It filled a void in my life and created an escape for me that I found thrilling and exciting. Granted in middle school, you're learing the basics of computer science and that was okay since a computer was a luxury we couldn't afford at first. In a way it was like learning a new language and exploring an unknown territory, a new world. It was than that I started to explore the possibilities of computing. I fell in love with it and the rest as they say is history .
